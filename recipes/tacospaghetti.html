Spaghetti with a Tex-Mex twist. So yummy everyone will come back for seconds. The best part is, it's cooked in one pot!!

Ingredients

  • 6 ounces spaghetti
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • ½ cup diced onion
  • 1 (10.75 ounce) can condensed cream of mushroom soup
  • 1 (10 ounce) can diced tomatoes and green chiles (such as RO*TEL®)
  • ⅔ cup water
  • 1 ounce taco seasoning
  • 1 (8 ounce) package processed cheese (such as Velveeta®), cubed
  • 1 cup shredded sharp Cheddar cheese

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Cook spaghetti in the boiling water, stirring occasionally, until tender yet firm to the bite, about 12 minutes.
  2. Heat a large skillet over medium heat and cook ground beef and onions until meat is browned and onions are soft and translucent, about 5 minutes. Stir in mushroom soup, tomatoes, water, and taco seasoning. Cook until well combined. Add Velveeta® and stir until melted, about 5 minutes.
  3. Drain spaghetti and add to skillet. Top with Cheddar cheese, cover, and let stand until cheese has melted.
